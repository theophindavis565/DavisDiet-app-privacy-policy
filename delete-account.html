Delete Your DavisDiet Account

Last updated: July 17, 2025

If you would like to permanently delete your DavisDiet account, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to:

Email: customercare@davisdiet.com

Include the following details in your email:

  • Your registered email or phone number
  • A short message like: “I would like to delete my DavisDiet account.”

What Happens Next?

Once we verify your identity, we will delete your account and all associated data from our system within 7 business days.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us at the same email address above.
